ITERATION 3:
The complete version of our website allows for people to upload a pet with an image and description for adoption. The poster can also edit or delete their posting, and all of their postings show up on the My Uploads page. Users can leave an inquiry about a pet on each pets page, and edit and delete their comments as well. When searching for a pet the users can filter by the type of pet that they are interested. Users can also favorite pets that will show up on the favorites page (if the user is logged in). Below are some photos showing the functionality of our website, PetFinder.
